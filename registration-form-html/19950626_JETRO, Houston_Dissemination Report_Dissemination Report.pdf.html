Dear
Sirs
,
I

"
JAPAN
TRADE
DIRECTORY
1995–96
”
(
JTD
)
.
As

invaluable

guide
,
this

Japanese

Japan
.
The
Japan

(
JETRO
)

free

,

the
world

,

JTD
has
been
complied
with
this

.
The
rise

domestic

in
Japan
for
imported

the
increased
number
of
companies
listed
who

import

.
The
information
contained
in
the
directory
covers
approximately
2,800
Japanese
companies
with
about
21,000
products
and
services
,
and
is
much
more
detailed
than
conventional
directories
.
In
addition
,
for
more
convenient
and
easier
access
to
relevant
information
,
the
new
edition
has
a
considerably
supplemented
index
structure
.
I
sincerely
hope
that
you
will
find
this
dir
ctory
useful
and
practical
,
and
that
it
will
make
positive

to
the
development
of
bilateral
trade
between
your
country
and
Japan
.

,
